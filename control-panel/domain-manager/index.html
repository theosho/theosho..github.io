When using the Domain Manager incorporated inside the Control Panel, you are able to take care of your domain names without hassles, within the same place you command your web sites. And also the Domain Manager is loaded with all the tools you need in order to properly deal with every aspect of your domain names.

Multi– Domain Control

All of your domains in one place

Via the Ktm Webhosting Control Panel, it’s possible to focus on a number of websites and domains all at once. Our Domain Manager makes it easy to regulate all the various elements that come with your domains – WHOIS details, name servers, custom DNS records, domain locking, etc. Additionally you can park limitless number of domains, reroute domain names, trigger Domain Info Protection, and more.

With just a click of the mouse, it’s possible to take care of your websites’ files, databases, mailboxes, or look at your existing site stats.

DNS Records

Complete DNS management

Through the Domain Manager user interface, you are able to speedily modify crucial DNS records for domains, registered or moved to your web hosting account. It will be possible to rather quickly set unique: A, AAAA, MX, SRV, CNAME, TXT, etc. records, adjust them at a later date as well as return all the way to the default values if you’re unhappy with a certain adjustment.

At any time, it is possible to register your own personal name servers – dns1.your–domain.com and dns2.your–domain.com to increase your brand name recognition amid users.

Domain Parking

Park your domains in only 1 step

Domain Parking is a wonderful solution that can be used to purchase attractive domain names and place them online with no need to have a website. It’s easy to park as many domain names as you want through the Domain Manager of your respective Control Panel. Parking a domain name will make it show an ’Under Construction’ web page, a "for sale" webpage, and even point the domain name to an alternative site.

And as soon as you’ve got your site completely ready, you can host back your domain name with merely a single mouse click! Your newly published site will come on the internet instantly.

Domain Redirection

Inbuilt domain re–direction

In order to redirect your domain to a different website url, it can be done with a click from the Domain Manager. We provide an easy to use tool for domain redirection. All you need to do is enter in the address where you choose to direct your domain name to. It is possible to forward it to an alternative domain within your web hosting account or to every other domain online.

And when you would want to forward the domain name straight back to your shared web hosting packages account – then click on the ’Host Domain’ button and our platform will perform what’s left.

WHOIS ID Protection

Keep your level of privacy

The Domain Info Protection solution is actually a good way to hide out the registration information and facts of your domain name from the online community. It will switch your personal info by means of generic company name and address, to make sure that not one person will be able to utilize it for unlawful purposes. If, however, an individual hopes to speak to you regarding your domain name, they’re able to still makes use of the listed email address, that can instantly divert the e–mail messsage for you.

SSL Certificates

Need to have for virtually every web store.

An SSL Certificate can offer your users an assurance that your particular site is safe and sound to generate online transactions or register as a member. It will secure pretty much all very sensitive info that can be transported involving the visitors and your web site and is going to block out all tries for misuse.

Through the Domain Manager in the Ktm Webhosting Control Panel, you can quickly get an SSL certificate for use on your online store or community site. Simply just choose if you want your SSL certificate for being for 1 or quite a few sites and then click the Request button. Your new SSL certificate is going to be hooked up on your behalf in a few minutes.
