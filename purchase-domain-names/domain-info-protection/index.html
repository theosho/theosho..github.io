WHOIS Protection with Ktm Webhosting

By default, your WHOIS info must be publicly available as well as utterly accurate. Therefore you need to put up your own or business data online and re–confirm that it is accessible for every person to look at. With the Domain Info Protection service provided by Ktm Webhosting, you’re able to cover up your entire information by applying our data as a substitute.
The best part is the fact that all domain–related emails (transfer requests, EPP codes, etcetera) are going to nonetheless go through, when undesired e–mail messages can be filtered out.

Whois Protection–eligible TLDs

Our company is unable to provide WHOIS protection for some of the domain names. It’s a restriction enforced by the organization responsible for managing the every specific TLD at the top level. This is exactly why only a limited number of domain names are now WHOIS protection–eligible:
The supported TLDs are: .com, .net, .org, .we.bs, .info, .biz, .co, .co.za, .me, .tv & .cc.

SSL Certificates

Get hold of SSL certificates from your CP

The simplest way to protect your online store is to activate an SSL certificate for your domain. The certificate will add ’https://’ to your domain name and will tell your potential clients that your online shopping portal is reliable enough to purchase from. Through the Domain Manager section of your Control Panel, you’ll be able to quickly request a conventional or a wildcard SSL certificate for your site.

All you have to do is push the Order SSL Certificate button, indicate the sort of SSL certificate that you want and fill in the required details. We will then get back to you with your SSL certificate’s details in a flash.

Quick Scalability

Upgrade to a cloud hosting plan immediately

You can upgrade the domain administration account that you have with us to one of our cloud hosting plans. In this way, you will be able to effortlessly administer both your domain names and websites from one single place. To enable all the web hosting options in your Control Panel, just go to the Upgrade Plan section located on the left and specify the plan that you want to upgrade to.

As soon as we activate the selected plan, you will get an array of tools, among them a drag ’n’ drop File Manager, a hotlink prevention tool, a domain name forwarding tool, an in–depth web site traffic stats tool, a 1–click Web Applications Installer using which you will be able to effortlessly launch personal weblogs, social networking websites, image galleries, and so on.

Various Custom DNS Records

A, AAAA, MX, CNAME records and more

From the Custom DNS section of the Domain Manager, you’ll be able to modify different types of DNS resource records for your domains, among them A, AAAA, MX, NS, SRV, CNAME and TXT resource records. Setting a new DNS resource record is amazingly easy. All you have to do is select a domain and then specify the DNS record that you want. Any modification you have made will be reflected live within one day. In case you want to retrieve the old DNS resource record value, simply hit the Revert button.

The Custom DNS section also offers a GeoIP location tool, which allows you to direct people from a particular geographic location to a certain language version of your website.
