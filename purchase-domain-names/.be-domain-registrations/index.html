Dot BE Domains

If you plan to construct a remarkable domain name for your site, Belgium’s country-specific extension – .BE, is an excellent selection in view of the fact that you can have an simple-to-remember expression in English. This will ensure you more visitors as it will be simple for them to go back to your site or to share it with kin, comrades and colleagues. A fashionable illustration is youtu.be, which is a shorter web address for the fashionable video sharing portal. You are able to book a .BE domain name through our firm and it will go online shortly after that. You will have complete control over the domain and will be able to create DNS entries, to park and forward your domain at any moment in time through the Hepsia Control Panel.

The .BE country-code domain extension was introduced in 1989 and loads of .BE domains have been obtained since then by foreign people, not by Belgians, as there is no local presence requirement for getting such a domain name. With the .BE domains that we provide, you can pick if you fancy to target the global Internet population or the local Belgian market, because such a domain is going to make it easier to improve your local search positions as well. Belgium is literally in the middle of the EU, which is the prime free-trade area in the world, a sizeable part of which consists of active Internet users. The registrations are valid for one year, existing domain names cannot be transferred between registrar companies and the Registrant contacts cannot be modified once the domain is acquired, so make sure that you provide correct information throughout the purchase process.
