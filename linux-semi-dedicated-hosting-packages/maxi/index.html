SSH Access

No charge SSH access for your server

With our Semi-dedicated 2 package you will get SSH access to the server, included at zero cost. Thus, it will be easy for you to promptly access the server’s terminal and communicate directly with the machine, without needing to utilize the Control Panel.

Due to the specific nature of Ktm Webhosting’s Linux semi-dedicated hosting packages, the SSH access will not give you server root rights. You’ll be able to to interact with your data files, databases, email and everything regarding your websites, however, you won’t be able to alter the server’s setup.
